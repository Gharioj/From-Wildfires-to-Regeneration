<ul class="doc-list">
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/governance-framework/' | relative_url }}">01 — Governance Framework</a>
  <p>Sets out the Framework's overall governance principles, structure and decision-making processes, and how these relate to strategic partners and patrons.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/community-stewardship-framework/' | relative_url }}">02 — Community Stewardship Framework</a>
  <p>Describes how the Framework engages the communities affected by wildfire and involved in regeneration, including participation, consultation and shared stewardship.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/legal-structure-paper/' | relative_url }}">03 — Legal Structure Paper</a>
  <p>Considers potential legal structures capable of supporting the Framework's long-term governance and operations, as a discussion paper rather than legal advice.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/operational-governance-framework/' | relative_url }}">04 — Operational Governance Framework</a>
  <p>Sets out the operational principles and mechanisms through which day-to-day activities are directed and coordinated among partners.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/financial-sustainability-framework/' | relative_url }}">05 — Financial Sustainability Framework</a>
  <p>Sets out the principles guiding the Framework's long-term financial sustainability, including funding, resource allocation and financial oversight.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/risk-management-framework/' | relative_url }}">06 — Risk Management Framework</a>
  <p>Establishes the principles and processes through which risks to the Framework are identified, assessed and managed.</p>
</li>
<li>
  <a class="doc-title" href="{{ '/governance/en/intellectual-property-and-open-knowledge/' | relative_url }}">07 — Intellectual Property and Open Knowledge</a>
  <p>Sets out the Framework's approach to intellectual property and open knowledge, recognising that the Framework aims to encourage adoption while preserving consistency and attribution.</p>
</li>
</ul>
