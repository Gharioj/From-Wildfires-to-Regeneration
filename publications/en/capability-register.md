<p>Framework Capability Register</p>
<p>Version 1.0</p>
<p>Strategic Partners Required for the Initial Deployment of the Framework</p>
<p>Supporting the Framework requires more than financial sponsorship. It requires a carefully selected network of Strategic Partners capable of providing the specialist expertise, engineering, technology, products and operational capability needed to establish the first fully integrated deployment.</p>
<p>No single organisation can deliver the Framework independently. However, collectively, the organisations identified within this register provide the core capabilities required to move from concept to implementation.</p>
<p>Each organisation has been selected not only for its technical capability, but also for its long‑term vision, organisational values, engineering excellence and ability to contribute meaningfully to the Framework beyond conventional sponsorship.</p>
<p>Together, these Strategic Partners represent the foundation upon which the first operational deployment can be delivered.</p>
<p>01</p>
<p>Environmental Intelligence</p>
<p>STRATEGIC PARTNER</p>
<p>CMCC Foundation</p>
<p>LOCATION Lecce, Puglia, Italy</p>
<p>OWNERSHIP Foundation</p>
<p>APPROXIMATE SCALE Approximately 700 staff.</p>
<p>WHY SELECTED</p>
<p>CMCC is internationally recognised for its expertise in climate science, wildfire modelling and environmental intelligence. Its presence in Lecce provides the Framework with immediate scientific credibility while maintaining a strong regional connection. Although not a commercial organisation, CMCC's contribution is considered fundamental to the Framework's scientific integrity.</p>
<p>FRAMEWORK CAPABILITY</p>
<ul>
<li>Climate modelling</li>
<li>Wildfire prediction</li>
<li>Environmental intelligence</li>
<li>Scientific validation</li>
<li>Data analysis</li>
<li>Long‑term environmental monitoring</li>
</ul>

<p>CONCLUSION</p>
<p>Collectively, these Strategic Partners provide the principal capabilities required to establish the Framework's first operational deployment.</p>
<p>Rather than functioning as independent suppliers, each organisation contributes a specialist capability that strengthens an integrated operational system.</p>
<p>Together they provide the engineering, technology, environmental expertise, infrastructure, communications and operational capability required to transform the Framework from concept into implementation.</p>
<p>The Framework is therefore not defined by any single organisation, but by the strength of the capabilities they collectively contribute.</p>
