<p>Operational Coordination</p>
<p>Operational coordination is what turns equipment, people and resources into a genuine capability for action. Without it, none of the elements described above can be implemented, coordinated or sustained over time.</p>
<p>A Patron does far more than fund equipment or individual projects. A Patron helps ensure that properly trained local people are in place precisely when the territory needs them most. In periods of lower fire risk, those same people turn from protecting the landscape to regenerating it — restoring ecosystems, strengthening environmental resilience, and building a lasting model of responsible land stewardship for future generations.</p>

<p>LEGACY RECOGNITION</p>
<p>Every operational capability funded — from thermal surveillance drones and Rapid Response Kits to Observation Stations and regeneration projects — may be dedicated in your name, in the name of your family or foundation, or in honour of a person of your choosing.</p>
<p>Your example may encourage other Patrons — families, foundations, family offices and philanthropic organisations — to support the protection and regeneration of Salento, extending the impact of the Framework for future generations.</p>
<p>Imagine children growing up in Salento, coming to know the vision and generosity of those who chose to act when it mattered most — protecting and regenerating the landscape they have the privilege of calling home.</p>
<p>Some opportunities are defined by the scale of the investment they require.</p>
<p>Others are defined by the significance of the moment in which they arise.</p>
