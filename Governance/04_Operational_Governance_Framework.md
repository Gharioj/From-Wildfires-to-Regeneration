# Operational Governance Framework

**Status:** Version 0.1 Working Draft

## Purpose

To set out the operational principles and mechanisms through which the day-to-day activities of the From Wildfires to Regeneration Framework are directed and coordinated.

## Scope

Covers operational roles, coordination between implementing partners, and routine decision-making. Does not cover strategic governance or long-term structural decisions, which are addressed in the Governance Framework.
