# Legal Structure Paper

**Status:** Version 0.1 Working Draft

## Purpose

To consider potential legal structures capable of supporting the From Wildfires to Regeneration Framework's long-term governance and operations.

## Scope

A discussion paper considering structural options and their alignment with the Framework's governance principles. This paper does not constitute legal advice, does not reach legal conclusions, and does not recommend a jurisdiction-specific course of action.
