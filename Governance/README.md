# Governance Documentation

## Purpose

This folder holds the governance documentation suite for the From Wildfires to Regeneration Framework. Together, these documents set out how the Framework is directed, how communities and partners participate in it, how it sustains itself, how it manages risk, and how it protects and shares its intellectual property as it is adopted more widely.

All documents in this suite are **Version 0.1 Working Drafts**. They establish structure and scope only, ahead of substantive drafting, and are intended for iterative refinement rather than immediate publication or external distribution.

## Documents

| Document | Summary |
|---|---|
| [01 — Governance Framework](01_Governance_Framework.md) | Sets out the Framework's overall governance principles, structure and decision-making processes, and how these relate to strategic partners and patrons. |
| [02 — Community Stewardship Framework](02_Community_Stewardship_Framework.md) | Describes how the Framework engages the communities affected by wildfire and involved in regeneration, including participation, consultation and shared stewardship. |
| [03 — Legal Structure Paper](03_Legal_Structure_Paper.md) | Considers potential legal structures capable of supporting the Framework's long-term governance and operations, as a discussion paper rather than legal advice. |
| [04 — Operational Governance Framework](04_Operational_Governance_Framework.md) | Sets out the operational principles and mechanisms through which day-to-day activities are directed and coordinated among partners. |
| [05 — Financial Sustainability Framework](05_Financial_Sustainability_Framework.md) | Sets out the principles guiding the Framework's long-term financial sustainability, including funding, resource allocation and financial oversight. |
| [06 — Risk Management Framework](06_Risk_Management_Framework.md) | Establishes the principles and processes through which risks to the Framework are identified, assessed and managed. |
| [07 — Intellectual Property and Open Knowledge](07_Intellectual_Property_and_Open_Knowledge.md) | Sets out the Framework's approach to intellectual property and open knowledge, recognising that the Framework aims to encourage adoption while preserving consistency and attribution. |
