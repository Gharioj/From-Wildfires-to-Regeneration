# Governance Framework

**Status:** Version 0.1 Working Draft

## Purpose

To establish the governance principles, structures and decision-making processes through which the From Wildfires to Regeneration Framework is directed and held accountable.

## Scope

Applies to the Framework's governing bodies, its decision-making processes, and its relationship to implementing partners, patrons and strategic partners. Does not cover day-to-day operational decisions, which are addressed in the Operational Governance Framework.
