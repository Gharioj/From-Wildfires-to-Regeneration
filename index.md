# From Wildfires to Regeneration

<p class="tagline">A framework for moving from wildfire crisis toward long-term ecological and social regeneration.</p>

From Wildfires to Regeneration brings together landowners, Custodians, local communities, Strategic Partners and Patrons in a shared effort to move from wildfire risk toward long-term ecological and social regeneration. This website is the public documentation portal for the Framework's Version 1.0 publication suite and its governance documentation.

The **GitHub repository remains the authoritative source** for every document presented here. This site exists to make that material easy to read, navigate and share.
